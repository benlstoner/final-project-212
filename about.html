Gyran Bop is a dish that originated in Korea, but doesn't have to be made traditionally Korean.

When I first started cycling, every morning before a ride I would have avocado toast with egg. Soon I found I would feel bloated and heavy during my ride. So I decided I needed to find another way to get the same carbohydrates, without all the filler.

Further research led me to find that most professionals eat omelletes with rice, due to the low fiber and high carb nature, along with fats and proteins from the egg. I later discovered that this dish is traditionally called "Gyeran Bap" from a Korean friend of mine.
